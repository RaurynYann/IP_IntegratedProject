Jewelry Care


1. Store jewelry in a cool, dry place in a box that comes with your piece.

2. Avoid direct contat with perfume, body lotions or other chemicals.

3. Clean the pieces after use and occasionally & wipe with a soft cloth. 4. Handle with care your jewelry should last for a long time.
