Accessories Care Tips

1. Accessories should be stored away SEPARATELY in an air-tight jewelry box.

2. Avoid direct contat with PERFUME,BODY LOTIONS or OTHER CHEMICALS.

3. CLEAN the pieces after use and occasionally & wipe with a soft cloth.
